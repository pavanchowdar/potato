INGREDIENTS

  • 1 royal red potato/brown potato
  • 5 cups cold water
  • oil for deep frying
  • 1/2 tsp pepper/kali mirch,crushed
  • pinch of salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. first,peel the skin of potato and slice.
  2. transfer the sliced potatoes into ice cold water and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. further,drain and pat dry over a kitchen towel.this prevents oil from spluttering.
  4. take the sliced potatoes one by one and deep fry.
  5. deep fry the sliced potato one by one deep fry
  6. deep fry the sliced potatoes in hot oil
  7. further,reduce the flame to medium and fry.
  8. stir occasionally and fry on both sides.
  9. once the potato slices turns crips and slight golden spots start to appear over the edges.
  10. further,drain off the potatoes over kitchen towel to remove excess oil.
  11. also immediately sprinkle some salit and pepper over chips and mix gently.
  12. Finally,potato chips are ready to serve or store in airlight container.
